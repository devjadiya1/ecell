What does E-Cell do?

E-cell provide a platform for students interested in entrepreneurship, offering guidance, resources, and networking opportunities to help them develop their business ideas and ventures. E-Cell SATI may organize workshops, seminars, and startup competitions to promote entrepreneurial culture and support innovation within the SATI community.

What steps do I need to take to become a member of E-Cell?

To join an E-Cell, contact the organization's coordinators through email or visit their designated space. Express your interest in joining and inquire about membership requirements. Typically, individuals passionate about entrepreneurship are welcome. Attend their events, workshops, and seminars to network and understand their activities better. Apply for membership if required, highlighting your enthusiasm and relevant skills. Engage actively once admitted, contributing to the entrepreneurial community.

What advantages do E-Cell members have?

The benefits of joining an Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell)-
1. Networking Opportunities.
2. Skill Developmen.
3. Mentorship and Guidance.
4. Access to Resources.
5. Exposure and Visibility.
